## Intro

The 30-60-90 Plan provides a structured roadmap for new team members' first 90 days, with specific goals, milestones, and expectations for each 30-day period. This plan ensures clear expectations, measurable progress, and successful integration into the team. Following this plan accelerates productivity and sets team members up for long-term success.

## Purpose

Establish clear expectations and milestones for new team members' first 90 days, enabling focused progress, measurable success, and effective performance management.

## When This Applies

- New team member onboarding
- Setting performance expectations
- Monitoring new team member progress
- Performance reviews and feedback
- Planning development and growth

## Process / Framework / Workflow

### 30-Day Plan: Learning & Foundation

**Goals:**
- Understand workstream processes and tools
- Complete initial training and onboarding
- Begin independent work with support
- Build relationships with team

**Key Activities:**
- Complete onboarding checklist
- Attend all training sessions
- Learn core processes and tools
- Shadow experienced team members
- Begin practice exercises
- Meet team members and build relationships

**Success Metrics:**
- Onboarding checklist 100% complete
- Can perform basic role functions with guidance
- Understands core processes
- Comfortable with primary tools
- Building relationships with team

**Deliverables:**
- Completed onboarding checklist
- Initial practice work completed
- Understanding of role and processes
- Relationships established

### 60-Day Plan: Application & Growth

**Goals:**
- Work independently on standard tasks
- Meet basic performance targets
- Contribute to team goals
- Continue learning and development

**Key Activities:**
- Work independently with minimal supervision
- Meet daily/weekly performance targets
- Handle standard situations effectively
- Seek feedback and apply learning
- Contribute ideas and improvements
- Continue skill development

**Success Metrics:**
- Meeting 80%+ of performance targets
- Working independently on standard tasks
- Handling routine situations effectively
- Contributing to team discussions
- Showing continuous improvement

**Deliverables:**
- Consistent performance meeting targets
- Independent work completion
- Contributions to team goals
- Continued learning and growth

### 90-Day Plan: Mastery & Integration

**Goals:**
- Meet full performance expectations
- Handle complex situations effectively
- Contribute significantly to team success
- Demonstrate mastery of role

**Key Activities:**
- Meet all performance targets consistently
- Handle complex and unusual situations
- Mentor or support other team members
- Contribute process improvements
- Take on additional responsibilities
- Demonstrate role mastery

**Success Metrics:**
- Meeting 100% of performance targets
- Handling complex situations effectively
- Contributing to team success significantly
- Demonstrating role mastery
- Ready for additional responsibilities

**Deliverables:**
- Consistent high performance
- Complex situation handling
- Process improvements or contributions
- Role mastery demonstration

### Performance Review Process

**30-Day Review:**
- Review onboarding progress
- Assess learning and understanding
- Identify areas needing support
- Set goals for next 30 days
- Provide feedback and guidance

**60-Day Review:**
- Review performance against targets
- Assess independence and growth
- Identify strengths and development areas
- Set goals for next 30 days
- Provide feedback and support

**90-Day Review:**
- Comprehensive performance review
- Assess role mastery
- Discuss career development
- Set long-term goals
- Plan for continued growth

## Role Responsibilities

**New Team Members**
- Follow 30-60-90 plan
- Meet milestones and goals
- Seek feedback and support
- Apply learning continuously
- Communicate progress and challenges

**Managers**
- Provide 30-60-90 plan
- Monitor progress regularly
- Provide feedback and support
- Conduct review meetings
- Adjust plan as needed

**Operations**
- Ensure consistent 30-60-90 plans
- Monitor overall onboarding effectiveness
- Optimize plans based on results
- Support managers and team members

## KPIs or SLAs

- **30-Day Milestones**: 100% complete
- **60-Day Performance**: 80%+ meeting targets
- **90-Day Performance**: 100% meeting targets
- **Retention Rate**: High retention through 90 days

## Example Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Successful 30-60-90**
New team member completes 30-day plan, meets learning goals. 60-day plan: meets 85% of targets, working independently. 90-day plan: meets 100% of targets, demonstrates mastery. Successful integration and performance.

**Scenario 2: Accelerated Plan**
Experienced hire accelerates through 30-day plan, reaches 60-day goals early. Plan adjusted, additional responsibilities added. Reaches 90-day mastery early. Plan adapted to experience level.

**Scenario 3: Extended Plan**
New team member needs additional support. 30-day plan extended, additional training provided. 60-day goals adjusted, more support given. Eventually reaches 90-day goals. Plan adapted to individual needs.
