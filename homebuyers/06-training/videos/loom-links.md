## Intro

This document provides links to Loom video recordings used for training, process walkthroughs, and knowledge sharing within the Everyday Homebuyers workstream. These videos enable asynchronous learning, process documentation, and consistent training delivery. Organized by category for easy access and reference.

## Purpose

Centralize access to all training and process videos, enabling team members to find and access relevant videos quickly. This supports learning, process understanding, and knowledge sharing.

## When This Applies

- Accessing training videos
- Learning new processes
- Reviewing process walkthroughs
- Sharing knowledge with team
- Onboarding new team members
