## Intro

Role Expectations define the responsibilities, performance standards, and success criteria for each role within the Everyday Homebuyers workstream. Clear role expectations enable team members to understand what's expected, managers to provide effective guidance, and the organization to maintain consistent performance standards. These expectations align with our mission, principles, and operational goals.

## Purpose

Establish clear, measurable expectations for each role, ensuring team members understand their responsibilities, performance standards, and how success is measured. This enables effective performance management and team alignment.

## When This Applies

- Setting performance expectations
- Performance reviews and feedback
- Role clarification and discussions
- Hiring and selection processes
- Career development planning

### SAR (Sales Acquisition Representative) Expectations

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Respond to inbound leads within 5-minute SLA
- Make outbound calls to new leads
- Qualify leads using standardized criteria
- Enter accurate data into CRM
- Route qualified leads to Acquisitions team
- Maintain communication with leads
- Follow daily workflow consistently

**Performance Standards:**
- **Response Time:** 100% of inbound leads within 5 minutes
- **Daily Contacts:** 50-75 contacts per day (calls, SMS, emails)
- **Qualification Rate:** 30%+ of contacted leads qualify
- **Data Quality:** 95%+ of leads have complete, accurate information
- **Pipeline Health:** Maintain target stage distribution
- **SLA Compliance:** 95%+ meet all SLAs

**Success Criteria:**
- Consistently meets performance targets
- Maintains high data quality
- Follows processes and procedures
- Contributes to team goals
- Demonstrates continuous improvement

**Growth Expectations:**
- Develops qualification skills
- Improves conversion rates over time
- Takes on additional responsibilities
- Mentors new team members
- Contributes process improvements

### Acquisitions Specialist Expectations

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Receive and review qualified leads
- Complete underwriting analysis (24-48 hours)
- Generate offers based on analysis
- Present offers to homeowners
- Negotiate terms and conditions
- Coordinate closing process
- Hand off closed deals to Properties

**Performance Standards:**
- **Underwriting Time:** 24-48 hours for standard properties
- **Offer Generation:** Within 4 hours of underwriting completion
- **Offer Acceptance Rate:** 15-20% of offers accepted
- **Deal Close Rate:** 60%+ of accepted offers close
- **Profit Margin:** Average meets target thresholds
- **SLA Compliance:** 95%+ meet all SLAs

**Success Criteria:**
- Consistently meets performance targets
- Maintains deal quality and profitability
- Follows underwriting processes accurately
- Handles negotiations effectively
- Contributes to team goals

**Growth Expectations:**
- Develops underwriting expertise
- Improves deal quality over time
- Takes on complex deals
- Mentors new team members
- Contributes process improvements

### Dispositions Specialist Expectations

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Manage buyers list and relationships
- Create deal packets for properties
- Contact buyers and present deals
- Manage showings and property access
- Evaluate offers and negotiate terms
- Coordinate closing process

**Performance Standards:**
- **Deal Packet Completion:** Within 48 hours of property ready
- **Buyer Response Rate:** 40%+ of contacted buyers respond
- **Average Offers:** 3-5 offers per property
- **Time to Sale:** Average 30-45 days
- **Sales Price:** Achieves target profit margins
- **SLA Compliance:** 95%+ meet all SLAs

**Success Criteria:**
- Consistently meets performance targets
- Maintains strong buyer relationships
- Creates effective deal packets
- Handles sales negotiations effectively
- Contributes to team goals

**Growth Expectations:**
- Develops sales expertise
- Grows buyers list
- Improves conversion rates
- Takes on complex sales
- Contributes process improvements

### Manager Expectations

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Oversee team performance
- Provide coaching and development
- Ensure process adherence
- Optimize team operations
- Manage escalations
- Coordinate cross-team collaboration

**Performance Standards:**
- **Team Performance:** Team meets collective targets
- **Individual Development:** Team members growing and developing
- **Process Adherence:** 95%+ process compliance
- **Escalation Handling:** 100% within SLA
- **Team Satisfaction:** High team satisfaction scores

**Success Criteria:**
- Team consistently meets targets
- Team members developing and growing
- Processes optimized and efficient
- Strong team culture and collaboration
- Contributes to workstream success

## Tools Used

- **Performance Tracking**: Monitor metrics and targets

## Role Responsibilities

**Team Members**
- Understand and meet role expectations
- Seek clarification when needed
- Work to exceed expectations
- Provide feedback on expectations
- Support team goals

**Managers**
- Communicate expectations clearly
- Monitor performance against expectations
- Provide feedback and coaching
- Adjust expectations as needed
- Support team member success

**Operations**
- Ensure consistent expectations
- Monitor expectation effectiveness
- Optimize expectations based on results
- Support managers and team members

## KPIs or SLAs

- **Expectation Clarity**: 100% of team members understand expectations
- **Performance Against Expectations**: 80%+ meeting expectations
- **Expectation Effectiveness**: Expectations drive desired performance
- **Team Satisfaction**: High satisfaction with expectations

## Example Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Clear Expectations**
New team member receives role expectations document. Reviews with manager, understands responsibilities and targets. Works to meet expectations, receives regular feedback. Meets expectations consistently. Clear expectations enable success.

**Scenario 2: Expectation Adjustment**
Team member consistently exceeds expectations. Manager reviews, identifies growth opportunities. Adjusts expectations, adds responsibilities. Team member continues to excel. Expectations adapted to growth.

**Scenario 3: Performance Issue**
Team member not meeting expectations. Manager reviews expectations, identifies gaps. Provides additional support and training. Team member improves, meets expectations. Expectations guide improvement.
