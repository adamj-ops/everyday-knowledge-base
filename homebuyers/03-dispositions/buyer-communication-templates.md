## Intro

Buyer Communication Templates provide standardized, effective messages for communicating with cash buyers throughout the sales process. These templates ensure consistent, professional communication while saving time and maintaining quality. Templates are designed to be personalized and adapted to specific situations and buyer preferences.

## Purpose

Establish a library of communication templates for common buyer interaction scenarios, ensuring consistent messaging while allowing for personalization. This enables efficient buyer communication and strong relationship management.

## When This Applies

- Initial property outreach to buyers
- Follow-up communications
- Scheduling showings
- Responding to buyer inquiries
- Negotiating terms
- Closing coordination

## Process / Framework / Workflow

### Communication Best Practices

**Tone:**
- Professional but friendly
- Clear and concise
- Value-focused (emphasize opportunity)
- Respectful of buyer's time
- Personal (use buyer's name)

**Timing:**
- Send during business hours
- Respond promptly (< 2 hours)
- Follow up appropriately (don't over-communicate)
- Respect buyer preferences

**Personalization:**
- Always use buyer's name
- Reference specific property details
- Mention buyer's preferences when relevant
- Adapt template to conversation context

### Template Categories

**Initial Property Outreach**

**Template 1: New Property - Email**
Subject: New Property Opportunity: [Property Address]

Hi [Buyer Name],

I have a new property that matches your buying criteria and wanted to share it with you first.

**Property Details:**
- Address: [Address]
- Type: [Single Family/Multi-Family/etc.]
- Size: [Square Footage], [Bedrooms]BR/[Bathrooms]BA
- Condition: [Condition Description]
- Asking Price: $[Price]

**Key Highlights:**
- [Key Feature 1]
- [Key Feature 2]
- [Key Feature 3]

I've attached a complete deal packet with photos, inspection reports, and financial projections. This property [selling point: great location, good ROI, quick close opportunity, etc.].

Would you like to schedule a showing? I'm available [times]. Let me know what works for you.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Phone Number]

**Template 2: New Property - SMS**
"Hi [Buyer Name], I have a new property at [Address] that matches your criteria. [Brief Description]. Asking $[Price]. Deal packet attached. Interested in a showing? [Your Phone Number]"

**Template 3: New Property - Phone Script**
"Hi [Buyer Name], this is [Your Name] from Everyday Homebuyers. I have a property I think you'll be interested in - it's [brief description] at [address], asking $[price]. It matches your preferences for [preference]. Would you like me to send you the deal packet?"

**Follow-Up Communications**

**Template 4: Follow-Up After Deal Packet**
Subject: Following Up: [Property Address]

Hi [Buyer Name],

I wanted to follow up on the property at [Address] I sent you earlier this week. Have you had a chance to review the deal packet?

This property [key selling points]. I have [number] other buyers interested, so if you're interested, I'd recommend moving quickly.

Would you like to schedule a showing or discuss the property? I'm available [times].

Best regards,
[Your Name]

**Template 5: Showing Confirmation**
Subject: Showing Confirmed: [Property Address] - [Date/Time]

Hi [Buyer Name],

Just confirming your showing for [Property Address] on [Date] at [Time].

**Property Access:**
- Address: [Full Address]
- Access: [Key location/Code/Contact]
- Contact: [Your Phone Number]

Please let me know if you need to reschedule or have any questions. Looking forward to hearing your thoughts after the showing!

Best regards,
[Your Name]

**Template 6: Post-Showing Follow-Up**
Subject: Following Up: [Property Address] Showing

Hi [Buyer Name],

Thanks for taking the time to view [Property Address] yesterday. What did you think?

I wanted to let you know that we've received [number] offers so far, with the highest at $[amount]. If you're interested, I'd recommend submitting an offer soon.

Would you like to discuss making an offer or have any questions about the property?

Best regards,
[Your Name]

**Accepted Terms:**
- Price: $[Amount]
- Closing Date: [Date]

Best regards,
[Your Name]

Thanks for your offer on [Property Address]. We appreciate your interest.

We've reviewed your offer and would like to counter with the following:

**Counter-Offer:**
- Price: $[Amount]

[Brief explanation if needed: "We can work with your timeline if we can adjust the price slightly" or "We have another offer at this price point"]

Best regards,
[Your Name]

### Template Usage Guidelines

**Personalization:**
- Always replace [Placeholders] with actual information
- Add personal touches based on relationship
- Reference specific details from previous interactions

**Adaptation:**
- Templates are starting points, not scripts
- Adapt tone and content to buyer and situation
- Add context or details as needed

**Timing:**
- Send during business hours
- Respond promptly (< 2 hours)
- Don't over-communicate
- Respect buyer preferences

## Tools Used

- **Email Platform**: Email templates and distribution
- **SMS Platform**: Text message templates
- **CRM System**: Track communications and templates used
- **Template Library**: Centralized template database

## Role Responsibilities

**Dispositions Specialists**
- Use templates as starting points
- Personalize templates appropriately
- Adapt templates to specific situations
- Track which templates work best
- Suggest new templates as needed

**Dispositions Manager**
- Maintain template library
- Review template effectiveness
- Update templates based on performance
- Train team on template usage

## KPIs or SLAs

- **Response Rate**: 40%+ response rate on initial outreach
- **Response Time**: Respond to buyer inquiries within 2 hours
- **Template Effectiveness**: Track open rates and responses by template
- **Buyer Satisfaction**: High marks for communication quality

## Example Scenarios

**Scenario 1: New Property Outreach**
Property ready for sale. Dispositions creates deal packet, identifies 15 matching buyers. Sends Template 1 (email) to all buyers. Follows up with Template 2 (SMS) to Tier 1 buyers. 8 buyers respond, 5 schedule showings.

**Scenario 2: Offer Negotiation**
Buyer submits offer. Dispositions evaluates, determines counter-offer needed. Uses Template 9 to present counter. Buyer accepts counter. Uses Template 8 to confirm acceptance. Proceeds to closing.

**Scenario 3: Closing Coordination**
Closing scheduled. Dispositions sends Template 10 with details. Sends Template 11 as reminder day before. After closing, sends Template 12 to thank buyer and maintain relationship.
