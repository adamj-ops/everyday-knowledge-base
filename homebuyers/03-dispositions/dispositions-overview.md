## Intro

The Dispositions function is responsible for selling properties after acquisition, managing relationships with cash buyers, creating compelling deal packets, and executing sales that maximize returns. This team transforms acquired properties into profitable sales by leveraging our curated buyers list, effective marketing, and strategic deal management. Dispositions ensures properties sell quickly at optimal prices, completing the acquisition-to-sale cycle.

## Purpose

Provide a comprehensive overview of the dispositions function, including processes, buyer management, deal packet creation, and sales execution. This serves as the foundation for understanding how properties are marketed and sold after acquisition.

## When This Applies

- Understanding the dispositions process
- Onboarding new dispositions team members
- Analyzing sales performance and conversion rates
- Optimizing buyer relationships and deal packets
- Coordinating handoffs from Acquisitions

## Process / Framework / Workflow

### Dispositions Workflow

1. **Property Receipt**: Receive property from Acquisitions or Properties team
2. **Property Assessment**: Review property details, condition, and rehab status
3. **Buyer Matching**: Identify appropriate buyers from buyers list
4. **Deal Packet Creation**: Prepare comprehensive deal packet with all property information
5. **Buyer Outreach**: Contact buyers, present deal, schedule showings
6. **Offer Management**: Receive and evaluate offers from buyers
7. **Negotiation**: Negotiate terms with selected buyer
8. **Closing Coordination**: Work with title company and buyer to close sale
9. **Post-Sale**: Complete documentation and handoff

### Key Activities

**Buyer Management:**
- Maintain and grow buyers list
- Categorize buyers by preferences
- Build relationships with key buyers
- Track buyer activity and preferences

**Deal Packet Creation:**
- Compile property information
- Include photos, inspection reports, repair estimates
- Provide financial projections and comps
- Create compelling presentation

**Sales Execution:**
- Market properties to buyers
- Manage showings and property access
- Evaluate and compare offers
- Negotiate terms and close deals

## Tools Used

- **Airtable CRM**: Buyer database, deal tracking, pipeline management
- **Buyers List System**: Organized database of cash buyers
- **Deal Packet Templates**: Standardized deal packet format
- **Email Platform**: Buyer communication and deal distribution
- **Photo Management**: Property photos and virtual tours
- **Title Companies**: Closing coordination
- **Communication Tools**: Phone, text, email for buyer communication

## Role Responsibilities

**Dispositions Specialists**
- Manage buyers list and relationships
- Create deal packets for properties
- Contact buyers and present deals
- Manage showings and property access
- Evaluate offers and negotiate terms
- Coordinate closing process

**Dispositions Manager**
- Oversee dispositions team performance
- Manage key buyer relationships
- Optimize deal packet effectiveness
- Analyze sales performance
- Develop buyer acquisition strategies
- Coordinate with Acquisitions and Properties teams

## KPIs or SLAs

- **Time to First Buyer Contact**: Within 24 hours of property ready
- **Deal Packet Completion**: Within 48 hours of property receipt
- **Buyer Response Rate**: 40%+ of contacted buyers respond
- **Offer Generation**: Average 3-5 offers per property
- **Time to Sale**: Average 30-45 days from property ready to closing
- **Sales Price**: Achieve target profit margins on sales

## Example Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Standard Disposition**
Property ready for sale after rehab. Dispositions creates deal packet, contacts 15 buyers from list. 8 buyers respond, 5 schedule showings. Receive 4 offers. Negotiate with top offer, close in 35 days. Property sold successfully.

**Scenario 2: Quick Sale**
Property in high-demand area, multiple interested buyers. Dispositions creates deal packet quickly, contacts buyers. Receive multiple offers within days. Accept best offer, fast-track closing in 21 days. Quick, profitable sale.

**Scenario 3: Challenging Property**
Property has unique features or challenges. Dispositions identifies specialized buyers, creates targeted deal packet emphasizing opportunities. Works with interested buyers, finds right fit. Eventually closes successfully.
