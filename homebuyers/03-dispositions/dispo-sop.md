## Intro

The Dispositions Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) provides step-by-step instructions for selling properties after acquisition, from property receipt through closing. This SOP ensures consistent, effective sales processes that maximize returns and maintain strong buyer relationships. Following this SOP enables quick sales at optimal prices while maintaining operational excellence.

## Purpose

Establish a standardized process for dispositions that ensures every property is marketed effectively, buyers are engaged properly, and sales are executed efficiently. This SOP serves as the definitive guide for dispositions team operations.

## When This Applies

- Processing any property ready for sale
- Training new dispositions team members
- Ensuring consistency across team
- Auditing sales processes for quality
- Optimizing dispositions workflows

## Process / Framework / Workflow

### Step 1: Property Receipt & Assessment (1-2 hours)

**Actions:**
1. Receive notification of property ready for sale
2. Review property details from Acquisitions/Properties
3. Verify property condition and rehab status
4. Review property photos and inspection reports
5. Understand property characteristics and positioning
6. Identify target buyer profile

**Deliverables:**
- Property assessment completed
- Target buyer profile identified
- Initial sales strategy determined

**Exit Criteria:** Property ready for deal packet creation

### Step 2: Deal Packet Creation (2-4 hours)

**Actions:**
1. Compile property information:
   - Property details (address, size, features)
   - Property photos (interior, exterior, key features)
   - Inspection reports (if available)
   - Repair estimates and rehab scope
   - Comps analysis and ARV
   - Financial projections
2. Create deal packet using template
3. Review packet for completeness and accuracy
4. Get manager approval if needed

**Deliverables:**
- Complete deal packet created
- All required information included
- Packet ready for distribution

**See Also:** [Deal Packet Template](./deal-packet-template.md)

### Step 3: Buyer Identification & Matching (30 minutes)

**Actions:**
1. Review buyers list for matching buyers
2. Filter by:
   - Property type preferences
   - Location preferences
   - Price range
   - Buying capacity
   - Activity level
3. Select 10-20 target buyers
4. Prioritize buyers (high-value, active, good fit)

**Deliverables:**
- Target buyers identified
- Buyer list prioritized
- Ready for outreach

**See Also:** [Buyers List System](./buyers-list-system.md)

### Step 4: Initial Buyer Outreach (Day 1)

**Actions:**
1. Send deal packet to target buyers via email
2. Follow up with phone calls to key buyers
3. Send SMS to buyers who prefer text
4. Track outreach and responses
5. Schedule showings for interested buyers

**Deliverables:**
- All target buyers contacted
- Responses tracked
- Showings scheduled

**See Also:** [Buyer Communication Templates](./buyer-communication-templates.md), [Email Sequence Template](./email-sequence-template.md)

### Step 5: Property Showings Management (Ongoing)

**Actions:**
1. Coordinate showing schedules
2. Provide property access (keys, codes, etc.)
3. Follow up with buyers after showings
4. Answer buyer questions
5. Track buyer interest levels

**Deliverables:**
- Showings coordinated effectively
- Buyer feedback collected
- Interest levels tracked

### Step 6: Offer Receipt & Evaluation (Ongoing)

**Actions:**
1. Receive offers from buyers
2. Document offer details:
   - Price
   - Terms
   - Closing timeline
   - Contingencies
   - Buyer qualifications
3. Compare offers:
   - Price comparison
   - Terms comparison
   - Buyer reliability
   - Closing certainty
4. Rank offers by overall value
5. Present top offers to manager if needed

**Deliverables:**
- All offers documented
- Offers evaluated and ranked
- Top offer identified

### Step 7: Negotiation (1-3 days)

**Actions:**
1. Contact top buyer(s) to negotiate
2. Discuss offer terms
3. Negotiate price, timeline, conditions
4. Finalize terms
5. Get buyer commitment
6. Document agreement

**Deliverables:**
- Terms negotiated and agreed
- Buyer committed
- Agreement documented

### Step 8: Closing Preparation (1-2 days)

**Actions:**
1. Execute purchase agreement with buyer
2. Order title work
3. Coordinate with title company
4. Schedule closing date (typically 14-30 days out)
5. Prepare closing checklist
6. Coordinate property access for final walkthrough

**Deliverables:**
- Purchase agreement executed
- Closing scheduled
- Closing checklist prepared

### Step 9: Closing Coordination (14-30 days)

**Actions:**
1. Monitor closing progress
2. Resolve any title issues
3. Coordinate final walkthrough
4. Address any buyer concerns
5. Prepare closing documents
6. Confirm closing date and time
7. Attend closing (if required)

**Deliverables:**
- Closing issues resolved
- Closing documents prepared
- Closing completed successfully

### Step 10: Post-Closing & Documentation (1 day)

**Actions:**
1. Update CRM with closing status
2. Complete all documentation
3. Update buyers list with transaction
4. Request buyer feedback
5. Celebrate success!
6. Analyze deal performance

**Deliverables:**
- Deal closed and documented
- Buyers list updated
- Performance analyzed

## Role Responsibilities

**Dispositions Specialists**
- Follow SOP for every property
- Complete all steps thoroughly
- Maintain buyer relationships
- Document work accurately
- Meet SLA timeframes

**Dispositions Manager**
- Ensure SOP adherence
- Review deals at key points
- Provide guidance and support
- Optimize SOP based on results
- Train team on SOP

## KPIs or SLAs

- **Deal Packet Completion**: Within 48 hours of property receipt
- **Buyer Outreach**: Within 24 hours of property ready
- **Offer Receipt**: Average 3-5 offers per property
- **Time to Sale**: Average 30-45 days from property ready
- **Closing Success**: 95%+ of accepted offers close successfully

## Example Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Standard Sale**
Property ready Monday. Deal packet created by Wednesday. 15 buyers contacted Thursday. 8 respond, 5 schedule showings. Receive 4 offers by following Monday. Negotiate with top offer, close in 35 days. Successful sale.

**Scenario 2: Quick Sale**
High-demand property, multiple interested buyers. Deal packet created quickly. Immediate buyer interest. Receive multiple offers within days. Accept best offer, fast-track closing in 21 days. Quick, profitable sale.

**Scenario 3: Challenging Sale**
Property has challenges, limited buyer interest. Expand buyer outreach, create targeted messaging. Work with interested buyers, find right fit. Eventually receive acceptable offer, close successfully.
