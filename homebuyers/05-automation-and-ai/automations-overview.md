## Intro

Automation and AI capabilities enable the Everyday Homebuyers workstream to operate at scale, reduce manual work, improve consistency, and accelerate processes. This overview covers the automation strategy, key automations in use, AI integrations, and how these technologies support operational excellence. Effective automation increases capacity, reduces errors, and frees team members to focus on high-value activities.

## Purpose

Provide a comprehensive overview of automation and AI capabilities within the workstream, including strategy, current implementations, and future opportunities. This enables understanding of how technology supports operations and identifies areas for expansion.

## Example Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Lead Response Automation**
Web form submitted. Automation captures lead, creates CRM record, assigns to SAR, sends notification. SAR receives notification within seconds, responds within 5-minute SLA. Automation tracks response time, updates lead stage. Significant time savings and consistency improvement.

**Scenario 2: Deal Packet Automation**
Property ready for sale. Automation generates deal packet: pulls property data, comps, photos, financials. Creates formatted packet, distributes to buyers list. Dispositions reviews, makes minor edits, sends. Saves 2-3 hours per property.

**Scenario 3: AI Communication**
LLM analyzes homeowner communication, suggests response tone and content. SAR reviews, personalizes, sends. Improves response quality and consistency. Saves time on routine communications.
