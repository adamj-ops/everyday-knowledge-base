## Intro

Compliance documentation outlines legal, regulatory, and ethical requirements that must be followed in the Everyday Homebuyers workstream. This includes real estate regulations, consumer protection laws, communication compliance (TCPA, CAN-SPAM), and ethical business practices. Maintaining compliance protects the business, ensures legal operation, and builds trust with homeowners and partners.

## Purpose

Document compliance requirements, regulations, and best practices to ensure legal and ethical operations. This protects the business and ensures team members understand and follow compliance requirements.

## When This Applies

- Understanding legal requirements
- Ensuring compliance in operations
- Training on compliance
- Handling compliance issues
- Reviewing compliance procedures

## Process / Framework / Workflow

### Key Compliance Areas

**Real Estate Regulations:**
- State and local real estate laws
- Licensing requirements
- Disclosure requirements
- Contract requirements
- Fair housing laws

**Consumer Protection:**
- Truth in Lending Act (TILA)
- Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA)
- State consumer protection laws
- Unfair and deceptive practices prohibitions

**Communication Compliance:**
- TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act)
- CAN-SPAM Act (email marketing)
- Do Not Call Registry compliance
- Text message consent requirements

**Data Protection:**
- Privacy laws
- Data security requirements
- Information handling procedures
- Consent and disclosure requirements

**Ethical Business Practices:**
- Honest and transparent communication
- Fair dealing with homeowners
- Accurate property representations
- Professional conduct standards

### Compliance Requirements

**TCPA Compliance:**
- Obtain consent before calling/texting
- Honor do-not-call requests
- Maintain do-not-call list
- Provide opt-out mechanisms
- Document consent properly

**CAN-SPAM Compliance:**
- Include accurate sender information
- Provide clear subject lines
- Include opt-out mechanism
- Honor opt-out requests promptly
- Monitor email practices

**Real Estate Disclosure:**
- Property condition disclosures
- Material facts disclosure
- Contract terms clearly explained
- Right to cancel disclosures
- State-specific requirements

**Fair Housing:**
- Non-discrimination in all activities
- Equal treatment of all parties
- Fair housing training
- Complaint handling procedures
- Documentation requirements

### Compliance Procedures

**Consent Management:**
- Document all consents
- Maintain consent records
- Honor opt-out requests
- Update consent status
- Regular consent audits

**Do-Not-Call List:**
- Maintain internal DNC list
- Check against DNC registry
- Honor all DNC requests
- Update list regularly
- Train team on DNC procedures

**Disclosure Requirements:**
- Provide required disclosures
- Document disclosure delivery
- Ensure understanding
- Answer questions
- Maintain disclosure records

**Complaint Handling:**
- Acknowledge complaints promptly
- Investigate thoroughly
- Respond appropriately
- Document resolution
- Learn from complaints

## Tools Used

- **Compliance Tracking**: Monitor compliance status
- **Consent Management System**: Track consents and opt-outs
- **DNC List Management**: Maintain do-not-call lists
- **Documentation System**: Store compliance records
- **Training Materials**: Compliance training resources

## Role Responsibilities

**All Team Members**
- Understand compliance requirements
- Follow compliance procedures
- Report compliance concerns
- Complete compliance training
- Maintain compliance in daily work

**Compliance Officer/Manager**
- Monitor compliance
- Provide compliance guidance
- Conduct compliance training
- Handle compliance issues
- Ensure compliance procedures

**Legal/Operations**
- Stay current on regulations
- Update compliance procedures
- Provide legal guidance
- Handle serious compliance issues
- Ensure overall compliance

## KPIs or SLAs

- **Compliance Training**: 100% of team members trained
- **Compliance Violations**: Zero violations
- **Consent Accuracy**: 100% accurate consent records
- **DNC Compliance**: 100% DNC list compliance

## Example Scenarios

**Scenario 1: TCPA Compliance**
Team member calls homeowner. Verifies consent on file, confirms not on DNC list. Makes call, documents interaction. Complies with TCPA requirements. No compliance issues.

**Scenario 2: Disclosure Requirement**
Property requires specific disclosure per state law. Team member provides disclosure, documents delivery, ensures understanding. Complies with disclosure requirements. Legal compliance maintained.

**Scenario 3: Compliance Issue**
Potential compliance concern identified. Reported to compliance officer. Investigated, addressed, procedures updated. Team trained on updated procedures. Compliance maintained, issue resolved.
